Our Services

Founded in 2019, Bento-Go provides customers a 'build your own meal,' bar-style experience where they get to fill their own bento box with their choice of rice, protein, and side ingredients. Bento-Go also provides catering services upon request, and although currently located in the New England region only, customers can look forward to new locations across the country -- coming soon!

Honoring the Japanese history of the bento box, our ingredients are all of the highest quality and are imported from Japan: all bought from local, small scale farmers.

Take a peak at our ingredient options:

Rice:

  • White rice
  • Brown rice
  • Cauliflower rice

Protein:

  • Grilled salmon
  • Karaage fried chicken
  • Pork katsu
  • Stir fry tofu

Sides:

  • Japanese potato salad
  • Japanese macaroni salad
  • Simmered kabocha
  • Sunomono
  • Harusame noodle stir fry
